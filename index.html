About

Heating world is about educating and making options available to our customers. We pride ourselves in making sure that each customer receives the best solutions for their particular heating needs at the best price possible. We also understand the importance of personalized service before, during and most importantly after the purchase. We will continue to research and bring forward the best quality products on the market to our area.

  • 1977

    Our Beginning

    Russ Krug, President and founder of Heating World, first started out with no more than a hand-painted piece of ply wood for a sign and three wood stoves in his basement. By 1978 Russ Krug and his wife Jeanne moved into their first shop, then known as “The World of Wood Stoves” when the shortage of oil revolutionized the need for wood stoves.

  • 1990

    Heating World

    “The World of Wood Stoves” became known as “Heating World” to address the wide variety of products actually sold. As the business developed, inventory grew to include coal, oil, gas, and pellet products which pushed further expansion for two more warehouses.

  • 2004

    Growing Heating World

    Russ and Jeanne were soon joined in daily operations by their son Brent. The Krug family became a team, growing the business into a dealership and distributor for many full product lines. With growth as a constant in 2008, a new show room was soon added to accommodate these products.

  • 2014

    Expanding Heating World

    Now Regionally recognized as one of the Largest dealers in central Pennsylvania, Heating World is still expanding the company to accommodate the arrival of gas in the region. With 5,000 square feet of showroom, we proudly display over 100 quality products as of today! And ensure to customers “We have it all”.
